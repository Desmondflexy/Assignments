About

Desmond Ikilo is a graduate of Mechanical Engineering from the University of Port Harcourt. He is from Benin City, Edo State but based in Abuja, Nigeria. He is passionate about coding and very fluent in Python programming language. He has also solved some Project Euler problems using Matlab, Python, C++, and Javascript. Desmond hopes to use his love and knowledge of Python and programming in general to build great apps and websites and become a master web developer.
